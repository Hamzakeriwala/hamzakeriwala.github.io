About

My name is Junaid Patel and I'm 21 years old. I study Computer Science at the University of Toronto, specializing in the Software Engineering stream. Being a co-op student, I worked as a Junior Web Developer in the law firm Osler, Hoskin & Harcourt LLP for 8 months in Winter 2014. Then, I worked as a Web Developer, again, for the Department of Historical Studies at UofT in Summer 2015. This position was under Dr. Shafique Virani, who is a distinguished professor at the University.

My ambition to become a programmer began in high school when I took Computer Science courses in grade 11 as well as grade 12. By developing simple Java programs, I found programming very intriguing and I slowly began having an affinity towards programming. This is when I decided to pursue a career in Computer Science and, from that point on, I've been working on projects in my own personal time as well as in University. From mobile apps to web pages, I'm always trying to think of new ideas that could benefit me or be of interest to others. My goal is to move up the "career ladder" and to grow in terms of skills.
